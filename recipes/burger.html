Burger

Description

A burger is a popular fast food item made by placing a cooked patty—usually made of minced meat like beef, chicken, or vegetables—between two halves of a soft, round bun. It is often layered with fresh ingredients such as lettuce, tomato slices, onions, and cheese, and topped with condiments like ketchup, mayonnaise, or mustard. The combination of a juicy patty, crispy vegetables, and flavorful sauces makes the burger both satisfying and versatile, allowing it to be enjoyed in countless variations around the world.

Beyond just being a quick meal, the burger has become a global culinary icon. From street food stalls to gourmet restaurants, it’s served in many forms — from the classic cheeseburger to modern plant-based versions. Burgers represent comfort, convenience, and creativity in food culture. Whether grilled at a family barbecue, ordered at a fast-food joint, or crafted with gourmet ingredients, a burger always delivers a delicious mix of textures and flavors that make it universally loved.

Ingredients

  • Burger Buns

  • 2 burger buns
  • 1 tbsp butter

  • Patty

  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1/4 cup boiled peas
  • 1/4 cup grated carrots
  • 2 tbsp bread crumbs
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • Salt(to taste)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala or mixed herbs
  • Oil(for shallow frying)

  • Toppings

  • 2 cheese slices
  • 2 lettuce leaves
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 small onion
  • 2-3 pickle slices or jalapenos

  • Sauces & seasoning

  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp mustard sauce
  • A pinch of black pepper or chili flakes

Steps

  1. Boil and Mash the Vegetables
  2. Season and Mix
  3. Shape and Cook the Patty
  4. Toast the Burger Buns
  5. Assemble the Burger
  6. Serve and Enjoy
